Some random fact about solar system

Venus

Venus is unusual because it spins the opposite direction of Earth and most other planets. And its rotation is very slow. It takes about 243 Earth days to spin around just once. Because it's so close to the Sun, a year goes so fast. It takes 225 Earth days for Venus to go all the way around the Sun. That means that a day in Venus is a little longer than a year on Venus.
